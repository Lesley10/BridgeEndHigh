Principal's Welcome

Welcome to Bridge-End High School As the Principal of Bridge-End High School, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our vibrant educational community. I am Mrs Nsor, Jennifer, and I bring over 20 years of experience in educational leadership and a deep passion for nurturing young minds. My academic journey includes a Masters in Education Administration from the University of Calabar and numerous accolades for innovative teaching practices and school administration. My commitment is to foster an environment where every student can achieve their fullest potential. Bridge-End High School, established on September 14, 2018, has grown into one of the leading secondary school institutions in Calabar and Nigeria at large, known for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education. Our school offers a rich blend of the Nigerian curriculum and looks forward to including international programs, preparing students for global opportunities. We are proud of our diverse student body and our team of dedicated educators who are committed to guiding our students to success. What sets Bridge-End High School apart from other institutions is our unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation.

Parents choose Bridge-End High School because we prioritize each student's individual growth, both academically and personally. Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention, and our comprehensive support services cater to the diverse needs of our students. We look forward to offering robust preparation for international examinations such as IGCSE, SAT, and TOEFL, ensuring our students are competitive on a global scale. Our inclusive and nurturing environment promotes a strong sense of community and belonging, making Bridge-End High School a second home for our students.
Our state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced science laboratories, a virtual art studio, and modern technical drawing and home economics labs, provide students with unparalleled learning opportunities. We integrate cutting-edge technology into our teaching methods and offer extensive extracurricular programs to ensure the all-around development of our Dear prospective students,
I am delighted that you are considering Bridge-End High School for your educational journey. Our school is a place where your dreams can take flight. Here, you will find a supportive community, dedicated teachers, and an array of opportunities to explore your interests and talents. At Bridge-End, we believe in the power of education to transform lives, and we are committed to helping you achieve your highest aspirations. We look forward to welcoming you to our family and supporting you every step of the way.
Warm regards
Mrs Nsor, Jennifer
Principal, Bridge-End High School
