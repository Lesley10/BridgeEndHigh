Meet the Principal

Welcome to Bridge-End High School As the Principal of Bridge-End High School, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our vibrant educational community. I am Mrs Nsor, Jennifer, and I bring over 20 years of experience in educational leadership and a deep passion for nurturing young minds. My academic journey includes a Masters in Education Administration from the University of Calabar and numerous accolades for innovative teaching practices and school administration. My commitment is to foster an environment where every student can achieve their fullest potential. Bridge-End High School, established on September 14, 2018, has grown into one of the leading secondary school institutions in Calabar and Nigeria at large, known for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education. Our school offers a rich blend of the Nigerian curriculum and looks forward to including international programs, preparing students for global opportunities. We are proud of our diverse student body and our team of dedicated educators who are committed to guiding our students to success.
